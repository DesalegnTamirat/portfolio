about me

Hi, I'm Desalegn Tamirat, a software developer from Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I graduated in Electrical and Computer Engineering and discovered my passion for programming during my early campus years, starting with C++ and Python. After graduation, I found my true interest in web development, especially full-stack development. I enjoy building user-focused applications and believe technology is a tool to connect people and create meaningful impact worldwide. I'm currently sharpening my skills in Node.js, TypeScript, and MongoDB while also mentoring others. My long-term goal is to join a major tech company and eventually start a tech company to empower Africa.
